Negotiation Timing Strategy

Pre-Announcement: Contract Review

Inventory all existing contracts, noting key terms and renewal dates

Post-Announcement: Initial Vendor Notification

Inform vendors of merger and request contract information

Prior to Day 1: First-Round Negotiations

Begin negotiations with vendors where immediate savings possible

30-90 Days Post-Merger: Strategic Negotiations

Conduct major negotiations with primary vendors after usage data available

Annual Renewal Cycle: Long-Term Planning

Review all contracts during annual renewal cycle for additional optimization
