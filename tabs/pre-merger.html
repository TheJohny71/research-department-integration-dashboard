Timeline Planning

3-6 Months Before: Initial Assessment

Form steering committee, sign confidentiality agreements, begin preliminary inventory

2-3 Months Before: Detailed Planning

Complete collection inventory, staff skills assessment, identify integration priorities

1 Month Before: Final Preparation

Review integration plan with leadership, finalize Day 1 activities, prepare communication

2 Weeks Before: Readiness Review

Conduct go/no-go assessment, confirm all teams are prepared for Day 1
